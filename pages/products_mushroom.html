Mushroom Details


Mushrooms are in different types. The most common types of mushroom are White button mushroom, Crimini mushroom, Portobello mushroom, Shiitake mushroom, Oyster mushroom, Enoki mushroom and so on.



Nutritional value of Mushroom:


Mushrooms contain protein, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. These can have various health benefits.
For example, antioxidants are chemicals that help the body eliminate free radicals.
Free radicals are toxic byproducts of metabolism and other bodily processes. They can accumulate in the body, and if too many collect, oxidative stress can result. This can harm the body cells and may lead to various health conditions.

Among the antioxidant, agents in mushrooms are:

  • selenium
  • vitamin C
  • choline


Health Benefits of Mushroom:


Mushrooms may help prevent lung, prostate, breast, and other types of cancer, according to the National Cancer Institute.
Dietary fiber in mushroom may help manage a number of health conditions, including type 2 diabetes.
The fiber, potassium, and vitamin C in mushrooms may contribute to cardiovascular health.
