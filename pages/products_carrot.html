Carrot Details


Carrots are root vegetables that were first grown in Afghanistan around 900 AD. Orange may be their best-known color, but they also come in other hues, including purple, yellow, red, and white.



Nutritional value of Carrot:


1 serving of carrot (1/2 cup) has,

  • 25 calories
  • 6 grams of carbohydrates
  • 2 grams of fiber
  • 3 grams of sugar
  • 0.5 grams of protein



Health Benefits of Carrot:


Beta-carotene in carrot helps protect your eyes from the sun and lowers your chances of cataracts and other eye problems. They can lower your risk of cancer, help your heart, boost your immune system , can help control diabetes and can strengthen your bones.
