Potato Details


Potatos are in different types. The most common types of potatos are Russet old potatoes, baking potatoes, Long white potatoes, also called white rose or California long whites , Yellow potatoes, Round white and Red potatoes, Purple potatoes or blue potatoes, also called sweet potatos and so on.



Nutritional value of Potato:


1 medium size white potato contains 200 calories and 0g fat. they also contain Vitamins C and minerals like pottassium and iron.



Health Benefits of Potato:


Vitamin C in potatos prevents degenerative diseases and premature aging. Pottassium in potatos helps lower high blood pressure.
